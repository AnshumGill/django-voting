SmartVote

We want to solve the problem with the existing voting model.
So we developed an online voting system utilizing Blockchain to make it secure.

Learn More

Reliable

Every vote is counted and cannot be changed, duplicated or removed.

Secure

This model avoids electoral fraud. The votes cannot be tampered with.

Cost-Effective

There is no need to arrange physical booths to hold elections. This will save a lot of time as well as money.

Anonymity

The voter remains anonymous before and after the election. There is no link between the voters' identities and ballots.

Verifiable

The system is verifiable to make sure all votes are counted correctly. After casting the vote, the voter can easily verify that their vote has been casted.

Easy to vote

There is no need to stand in long queues at the voting booth. Voting can take place at the comfort of your home.

How it works

Register yourself as a voter by providing your personal details. This is a necessary step for casting your vote for an election.

Log into your account during an ongoing election to cast your vote.

Click on the 'Vote' tab and enter your city and locality. Then select the candidate you want to vote for. Enter the OTP sent to your phone to finally cast your vote.

Click on the 'Verify Vote' tab to verify that your vote has been casted and counted.
